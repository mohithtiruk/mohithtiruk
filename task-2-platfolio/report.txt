For this foundational portfolio, I chose GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow to establish my professional digital footprint. GitHub serves as my technical laboratory; it is where I host my code, collaborate on open-source projects, and showcase my version control skills. LinkedIn acts as my living resume and networking hub, allowing me to connect with industry professionals and track internship opportunities. Finally, Stack Overflow is my primary resource for community-driven problem-solving and technical troubleshooting.

Over the next four years, I plan to use these platforms as a roadmap for my growth. On GitHub, I will maintain a "project-a-month" habit to build a robust repository of work. On LinkedIn, I intend to share monthly updates regarding my certifications and academic milestones to build a "personal brand" before graduation. By consistently contributing to Stack Overflow, I hope to transition from a learner who asks questions to a contributor who provides solutions. Collectively, these platforms will ensure that by my final year, I have a documented history of technical competence and professional engagement that distinguishes me from other candidates.
